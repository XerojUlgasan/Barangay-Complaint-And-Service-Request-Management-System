# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Barangay Complaint and Service Request Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline communication and service delivery between barangay (village) officials and residents in the Philippines. The system digitizes traditional paper-based processes for filing complaints, requesting certificates, and managing community announcements.

## Value Proposition
- **For Residents**: Easy online submission of service requests and complaints without visiting the barangay hall
- **For Officials**: Centralized dashboard to track, manage, and respond to resident requests efficiently
- **For Administrators**: Complete oversight of all operations with user management and analytics capabilities

## Key Features

### Resident Portal
- **Service Requests**: Submit certificate requests (Barangay Clearance, Certificate of Indigency, etc.)
- **Complaint Filing**: Report incidents, issues, or concerns to barangay officials
- **Request Tracking**: Monitor status of submitted requests (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
- **Announcements**: View barangay announcements and community updates
- **Dashboard**: Overview of all requests and complaints with status counts

### Official Portal
- **Request Management**: Review and process resident service requests
- **Complaint Handling**: Address and resolve filed complaints
- **Announcement Creation**: Post announcements for residents
- **Dashboard Analytics**: View statistics and pending items requiring attention

### Admin Portal
- **User Management**: Manage resident and official accounts
- **Request Oversight**: Monitor all service requests across the barangay
- **Announcement Management**: Create, edit, and delete announcements
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Comprehensive statistics on system usage and request trends
- **System Administration**: Full control over platform operations

## Target Users

### Primary Users
1. **Residents**: Community members who need barangay services or want to file complaints
2. **Barangay Officials**: Staff members who process requests and manage complaints
3. **Barangay Administrators**: Leadership with full system access and oversight

### Use Cases
- Resident requests a Barangay Clearance certificate for employment requirements
- Resident files a noise complaint about a neighbor
- Official reviews pending certificate requests and approves them
- Administrator posts emergency announcements to all residents
- Administrator generates reports on service request trends
- Official updates complaint status as investigation progresses
